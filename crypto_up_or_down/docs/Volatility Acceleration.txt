e best signal for detecting upcoming Polymarket mispricings during volatility spikes comes from a simple structural fact:

Polymarket updates slowly because humans price it.
Crypto markets update instantly because algorithms price them.

So the exploitable signal is real-time volatility expansion in the underlying asset.

Professional traders monitor this using realized volatility acceleration.

The Signal: Volatility Acceleration

Not just “volatility high”, but volatility suddenly increasing.

Compute:

volatility_ratio = short_term_vol / baseline_vol

Where:

short_term_vol = realized volatility over last 5–15 minutes
baseline_vol   = realized volatility over last 24 hours
Signal Trigger
volatility_ratio > 2

Meaning short-term volatility is double the normal level.

When this happens:

Polymarket probabilities lag

because traders react manually.

Why This Creates Mispricing

Imagine this scenario.

BTC moves:

+3.5% in 8 minutes

Prediction market traders will:

read Twitter

think about implications

manually place trades

Meanwhile your model instantly recomputes:

P(BTC > strike)

This lag creates temporary mispricing windows.

Typical duration:

2–15 minutes

Which is huge in trading terms.

Example

Market:

BTC > $80k by Friday

Before move:

Variable	Value
BTC price	77k
Volatility	55%
Probability	0.31

Now BTC jumps to:

79.5k

New probability might be:

0.54

But Polymarket may still show:

0.36

Edge:

18%

These happen all the time during crypto spikes.

The Key Insight

Probability is very sensitive near the strike.

When price approaches the strike:

dP/dS becomes large

Meaning small price moves cause huge probability shifts.

Retail traders underestimate this.

Professional Signal Stack

A desk would combine three indicators.

1. Real-Time Volatility Spike
vol_5min / vol_24h > 2
2. Distance to Strike
abs(price - strike) < 5%

Closer = stronger signal.

3. Time Remaining

Best window:

4 hours – 3 days

Short enough for volatility to dominate.

When All Three Align

This is the sweet spot.

Example:

BTC = 79.2k
Strike = 80k
Time = 36h
Vol spike detected

Probability will shift dramatically.

Prediction markets usually lag.

Even Better Signal: Orderbook Imbalance

Another early indicator:

Large aggressive buying or selling on exchanges.

Compute:

orderbook_imbalance =
(bid_volume - ask_volume) /
(bid_volume + ask_volume)

If:

imbalance > 0.6

Strong directional pressure likely.

Your model anticipates the move before it happens.

Polymarket traders will react only after price moves.
